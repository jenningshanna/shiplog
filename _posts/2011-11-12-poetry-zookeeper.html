--- layout: post title: "Zoo Keepers Manifesto" date: 2011-11-12 categories: fun poetry --- this is in short the existence
of two breaking gently.
the perspective of the past
is at best, shaking –
unresponsive to the traditional reaction
in a way that only the pair can explain.
while coinciding with one another,
the objects of desire are kept
behind lock and key,
for without,

there’d be no reason for me.
